Custom 3D Printed Phone Case

Overview

This project showcases a 3D-printed phone case for the iPhone 16 Pro Max, designed for optimal protection and usability. By leveraging TPU material and parametric modeling, the case ensures a snug fit, button accessibility, and customizable aesthetics.

Design Features

  • TPU Construction: Flexible and impact-resistant for daily use.
  • Button Flaps: Thin responsive layers for effortless pressing.
  • Raised Lip: Protects screen when face-down.
  • Detachable Camera Cover: Allows easy flat-bed printing and lens protection.
  • SVG Personalization Slot: Add your own design elements.

Design & Iterative Process

The design evolved through several iterations to improve fit, usability, and customization:

  • Initial Prototype: Based on Apple schematics; fit was too tight.
  • Refined Model: Improved tolerances, better button alignment, and thinner walls.
  • Enhanced Button Accessibility: Optimized button flap design and refined personalization slot.
  • Final Version: Integrated all feedback into a polished, functional case.

Conclusion

This custom phone case demonstrates how 3D printing can provide a cost-effective, personalized alternative to mass-produced cases. By utilizing TPU and parametric modeling, the final product achieves an ideal balance between durability, flexibility, and customization.
